  • Tioga Road (Highway 120 through the park) and Glacier Point Road are closed for the winter

    Alert 1, Severity closure, Tioga Road (Highway 120 through the park) and Glacier Point Road are closed for the winter
    Tioga Road (continuation of Highway 120 through the park) and Glacier Point Road are closed for the season due to snow. They usually open around late May or June. Call 209/372-0200 (then 1, 1) for road conditions and tire chain requirements.

Food Options

Food options in Yosemite National Park are managed by Yosemite Hospitality, and range from groceries to pizza to a fine dining experience at The Ahwahnee. Dining reservations are available 366 days in advance for The Ahwahnee Dining Room, the Mountain Room, Tuolumne Meadows Lodge, and White Wolf Lodge and are recommended, especially from spring through fall and during holidays.

 

Food In Yosemite Valley

 
The Ahwahnee Dining Room

A fine dining experience in a grand banquet hall, which also hosts a Sunday brunch buffet and special events year-round.

The Ahwahnee Bar

Indoor and outdoor seating available for drinks, hot cocoa, and snacks in the lobby of the Ahwahnee year-round.

Base Camp Eatery

This year-round food court at the Yosemite Valley Lodge offers a variety of breakfast, lunch, and dinner selections and a Starbucks bar.

Mountain Room

A upscale casual dining experience open for dinner year-round at the Yosemite Valley Lodge, with great views of Yosemite Falls.

Mountain Room Lounge

Appetizers, light fare, drinks, and a firelight ambiance are available year-round, located at the Yosemite Valley Lodge.

Village Store

Located centrally in Yosemite Village, the store carries a large selection of groceries, ready-to-eat foods, firewood, and camping supplies.

Degnan's Bakery

Sandwiches, salads, pizza, coffee, and breakfast items are available a la carte in Yosemite Village. Open for breakfast & lunch year-round.

The Loft at Degnan's

Pizza, rice bowls, BBQ, salads, and drinks available for lunch and dinner year-round at this casual upstairs joint in Yosemite Village.

Village Grill

Grilled sandwiches, burgers, hot dogs and veggie options can be enjoyed at outdoor seating next to the Village Store; open seasonally.

Curry Village Pavilion

A variety of casual food options are available year-round at a large, cozy indoor space.

Curry Village Pizza Patio & Bar

Pizza, salads, and drinks are available for lunch and dinner; open seasonally with outdoor seating.

Meadow Grill

Grab burgers, chicken and fish sandwiches, salads, and veggie options at this outdoor food option in Curry Village; open seasonally.

 

Food Outside Yosemite Valley

 
Wawona Hotel Dining Room

Victorian architecture provides the background for a relaxing meal in Wawona, near the Mariposa Grove of Giant Sequoias. Open seasonally.

White Wolf Lodge Dining Room

Buffet-style breakfasts and family-style dinners available at the White Wolf Lodge along Tioga Road; open seasonally.

High Sierra Camps

Hikers staying at the High Sierra Camps or in nearby backpackers' campgrounds can enjoy fresh-prepared dinners, breakfasts, and box lunches.

Tuolumne Meadows Lodge

Family-style dinners are served seasonally in a central tent near the Tuolumne River. Reservations required.

Tuolumne Meadows Grill

Grab hamburgers, hot dogs, chili, and more at this seasonal food option near popular trailheads in Tuolumne Meadows.

Markets and Gas Stations

Yosemite Hospitality operates small year-round food markets and gas stations in El Portal, Crane Flat, and Wawona.
